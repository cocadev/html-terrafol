How does the Terrafol.io Platform work?

Terrafol.io allows its users to participate in direct real estate transactions through acquiring interest in pre-determined, functional units of real estate assets. Our consensus mechanism enables owners to make collective decisions regarding each property they own units in, which are implemented by the asset managementcompany.

How do I exit?

Property Sale

A set time limit and a certain percentage increase in value of an asset are determined as the criterion for an exit, and either one of the conditions being satisfied triggers the trustee to actively pursue the sales of the asset. The registered users of the platform also have the ability to collectively decide to either accept or reject purchase offers received by the trustee based on predetermined criterion set for an exit.

Secondary listing platform

The users can at any point list their respective units in the property for sale on secondary listing platform.
