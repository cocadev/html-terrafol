FAQ

General

What is Terrafol.io?

Terrafol.io is an online real estate advertising / marketing and information services platform connecting users with the real estate market.

How are the real estate assets selected?

Terrafol.io specialist property executives conduct enhanced due diligence, on potential real estate assets (“Assets”)in order to assess them against our rigorous key criteria requirements. If an Asset meets the requisite, pre-determinedstandardsit will be included in our curated assets list on Terrafol.io.

How doI stand to benefit?

Youstand to benefit through two potential avenues; capital appreciation and rental income.

How is Terrafol.io different from the existing players in the market?

Terrafol.io allows you to choose the Asset/unit of the Asset in which you purchase beneficial interest and have control of day to day operations through a direct arrangement with an asset management company.

What is Terrafol.io’s role?

Terrafol.iois merely an online real estate advertising / marketing and information services platform connecting users with the real estate market and does not own the Assets. Your ownership of beneficial interest in the Assets is independent of the financial position of Terrafol.io.

What is the process for registration?

A user of Terrafol.io, who wishes to purchase beneficial interest in an Asset must complete the verification and KYC process in order to complete the process for registration (“Registered User”).

Terrafol.io Experience

The Terrafolio team has a wealth of experience in the real estate industry and every property that is listed on the platform has gone through a vigorous selection process to ensure only grade A real estate opportunities are available to our users.

Terrafol.io Structure

All beneficial ownership purchases are facilitated by the means of a trust, in which our users are beneficiaries. The conditions of the trust are pre-determined and made known to users before any transaction takes place, and any payments to the trust are held in escrow before any completion of sale. Our sales process is structured this way to ensure that Terrafolio never has control over your funds, which safeguards our users from any issues that could arise.

Why Terrafolio?

Terrafolio provides a mechanism for receiving capital appreciation and rental income on real estate purchases as low as 5 lahk rupees, which can allow users access to the real estate market for the first time or facilitate the diversification of experienced real estate portfolio. The experienced Terrafolio team ensures only grade A real estate opportunities are available to our users, and the management of the properties is taken care of by our partnered management company, meaning users only have to monitor their returns. The combination of our easy to use platform and real estate experience provides users the ability and confidence to participate in the real estate market.

What is the difference between Traditional purchases and Beneficial ownership?

A traditional purchase occurs when you are purchasing the whole property, and taking over all obligations associated management of the property and the legal title. Beneficial ownership purchasesallow for the purchase of property in small ticket sizes.

Property

Who takes care of tenancy and management?

A Registered User of Terrafol.io enters into an agreement with an asset management company to handle both tenancy and general property management. When a registered user of Terrafol.io purchases beneficial interest in an Asset, a Digital Receipt (“DR”) is issued to him, as a proof of his payment for beneficial interest. Any major decisions are through the consensus of DR owners in proportion to their ownership of beneficial interest in the Asset.

Of course, you can contact us and we will help facilitate this.

Will I be notified about new real estateopportunities?

Do I choose the properties I purchase?

The Terrafolio platform provides you with a selection of real estate opportunities with indicated capital growth and rental yields, then based on your own judgement, you decide which opportunity is appropriate for you. However, users can always contact a Terrafolio Executive for more information on any properties and viewings can be arranged to provide you with more insight into the property.

Where do the properties come from?

Our properties are sourced from either our vast network of trusted real estate developers, that has been formed over years of working in the real estate industry, or through developers applying to list their properties on the platform. All properties must go through our selection process before being added to our curated list of real estate assets.

Can I purchase multiple properties at the same time?

One of the key benefits to using the Terrafolio platform is the ability to diversify your portfolio, so we encourage users to purchase multiple properties and reduce the risk of purchasing property. Our platform dashboard will enable you to analyze your portfolio based on location, property type and see the resultant capital growth and rental incomes, easing the creation of a diversified portfolio. Terrafolio provides users with all the necessary information to make an educated purchases that suits you, but it is recommended to do independent market research before making any purchase.
